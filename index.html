The engine I use for my games is Scratch. Now before you get your pitchforks, I do not like Scratch. Well, baseline Scratch, that is. Since Scratch is open-source, there are plenty of modifications of the engine out there, so I do use Scratch, but only a modified version of it. Called Turbowarp, which makes projects run faster and has extensions like having gamepad support and APIs. But I understand that Scrath is for begining programmers, after LOST COLONY, I will look for different game engines. Becuase I am simply getting tired of Scratch.
